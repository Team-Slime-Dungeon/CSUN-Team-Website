Introduction

The Purpose of Our Game

The purpose of developing this roguelike dungeon crawler game is to provide both new and experienced players a new and engaging gaming experience. Not only will players be able to constantly challenge themselves with every new level, players will also be able to fully immerse themselves with the captivating storyline and characters. This roguelike dungeon crawler is designed to be both pleasing to the eye and to the ear as the environment is created with vibrant colors and the sound/music is made to further engage the players.


For the developers, this is an opportunity to challenge themselves and further develop their skillsets. It's a platform in honing skills such creativity, critical thinking, team work, coding, and design.
